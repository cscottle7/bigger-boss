## Page Layout Philosophy

### User-Centred Design Principles

#### 1. Empathy-First Approach
Every page layout acknowledges the emotional state of legal service users:
- **Stress Reduction**: Clean, uncluttered layouts reduce cognitive load
- **Trust Building**: Professional credibility markers throughout
- **Clarity Focus**: Clear navigation and obvious next steps
- **Support Availability**: Multiple contact options prominently displayed

#### 2. Information Hierarchy
Strategic content prioritisation based on user needs:
- **Primary Information**: What users need to know first
- **Secondary Details**: Supporting information and context
- **Action Items**: Clear calls-to-action and next steps
- **Trust Signals**: Credentials, testimonials, and local expertise

#### 3. Conversion Pathway Optimisation
Every page designed to guide users toward consultation:
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Information revealed as users become more engaged
- **Multiple Conversion Points**: Various opportunities to request consultation
- **Risk Reduction**: Free consultations and transparent pricing
- **Social Proof**: Client testimonials and success indicators

## Accessibility & Compliance Standards

### WCAG 2.1 AA Compliance

#### Visual Accessibility
**Colour and Contrast:**
- Minimum contrast ratio 4.5:1 for normal text
- Minimum contrast ratio 3:1 for large text
- Colour not used as sole means of information
- Alternative text for all images and icons

**Typography:**
- Minimum 16px font size for body text
- Maximum line length 80 characters
- Adequate line spacing (1.5x minimum)
- Sans-serif fonts for digital accessibility

#### Navigation Accessibility
**Keyboard Navigation:**
- All interactive elements keyboard accessible
- Logical tab order throughout pages
- Skip navigation links for screen readers
- Focus indicators clearly visible

**Screen Reader Compatibility:**
- Semantic HTML structure with proper headings
- Alternative text for images and graphics
- Form labels properly associated
- ARIA landmarks for page structure

#### Interactive Accessibility
**Form Accessibility:**
- Clear form labels and instructions
- Error messages clearly associated with fields
- Required fields properly indicated
- Success confirmations provided

### Legal Industry Compliance

#### Professional Standards
**Australian Legal Professional Standards:**
- Clear fee disclosure and transparency
- Professional indemnity insurance display
- Proper legal disclaimers
- Client confidentiality assurance

**Advertising Standards:**
- No misleading claims or guarantees
- Appropriate use of testimonials
- Factual accuracy in all content
- Professional presentation standards
